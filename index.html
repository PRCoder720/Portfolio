About Me

Hi, I’m Philip Ross, a web developer, designer, and AI integration specialist based in Denver, Colorado. With a background in both web development and AI, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to every project. I specialize in crafting dynamic, responsive websites and integrating powerful AI solutions that enhance functionality and user engagement.

Whether it’s building a sleek corporate website, developing an AI-driven tool, or creating a vibrant e-commerce platform, I focus on delivering solutions that are both visually stunning and highly effective. My approach is rooted in collaboration, ensuring each project is tailored to meet the unique needs and goals of my clients.

By combining cutting-edge design with innovative technology, I help businesses create impactful online experiences. If you’re looking for a professional who can bring your web and AI visions to life, let’s work together to make it happen!

In my free time, I enjoy volunteering with local charities, giving back to the community through meaningful projects. As a passionate snowboarder, I love hitting the slopes in the Rockies and sharing that passion with others. I volunteer with the Burton Chill Program, where I teach underprivileged kids how to snowboard, helping them build confidence and discover the joy of the sport.

I’m also passionate about traveling, exploring new cultures, and learning from every destination I visit. Recently, I’ve had the opportunity to explore Japan, Canada, Mexico, and Italy, as well as frequent visits to New York and California. These experiences broaden my perspective and spark fresh ideas for my work. They play a big role in shaping my creative process, inspiring me to craft digital solutions that are as unique and dynamic as the places I’ve explored.

Technical Expertise

I bring a versatile and comprehensive skill set in web development, AI, and cloud technologies. My core competencies include:

  • Front-End Development: Proficient in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and React, enabling the creation of dynamic, responsive, and visually appealing user interfaces.

  • AI and Machine Learning: Skilled in leveraging ChatGPT, OpenAI, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to develop innovative, AI-driven solutions.

  • Back-End Development & Databases: Experienced in working with SQL and NoSQL databases to design scalable, efficient data structures and support seamless functionality.

  • Cloud Computing & Hosting: Knowledgeable in cloud infrastructure and hosting solutions, ensuring secure and reliable deployment of web applications.

  • Project Management: Adept at applying Agile methodologies to streamline development workflows and deliver high-quality projects efficiently.

  • File & Data Management: Proficient in working with JSON and FTP for seamless data exchange and integration.

  • Advanced Tools: Familiar with Gemini for specialized tasks, optimizing both development and integration processes.

This diverse skill set allows me to approach projects with a holistic perspective, ensuring every solution is robust, innovative, and tailored to client needs.
